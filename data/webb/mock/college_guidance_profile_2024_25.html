College Guidance Profile 2024-25

Webb is an independent college preparatory school in Southern California with boarding and day students in grades 9–12. Ninety-nine percent of graduates gain admittance to selective four-year colleges and universities throughout the U.S. and abroad. One percent choose an alternative to the four-year path. This commonly includes a gap year. Webb provides a superior academic and residential experience and a highly rigorous academic program. Our students participate in leadership, residential, athletic, and advisory programs while experiencing all the benefits of living and learning in a diverse and supportive community.
