Sharing Our Values

Structured fixture content for the Webb student life page.

Overview

After the day's first lesson, the community gathers in Vivian Webb Chapel to hear that morning's Chapel Talk.

Seniors are invited to craft a Chapel Talk to share what they have learned during their time at Webb with their peers, faculty, staff, family, and special guests.

Seniors deliver their talks in gratitude. They often express their growth and development at Webb-growth gained through failure and success.

Talks range from lighthearted to deeply philosophical and serve as a forum for seniors to share lessons learned with their younger peers and the entire community.
