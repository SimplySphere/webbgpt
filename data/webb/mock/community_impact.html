Webb Students Serve the Pomona Valley Community

Structured fixture content for the Webb student life page.

Dec 12, 2025

Recently, Webb students took part in a community service outing in Pomona, partnering with local organizations to support families in need. Working alongside Helping Hands at Calvary Fellowship Church, students helped organize and distribute grocery items to more than 250 community members.

The morning was filled with meaningful work, teamwork, and connection. Students contributed their time and energy-from early-morning setup to hands-on distribution-demonstrating a strong commitment to service and care for the wider community. The experience concluded with a shared meal, offering time to reflect and build relationships.

This effort reflects a core Webb value: taking action to make our communities stronger, more supportive, and more connected.
